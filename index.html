Health & Entry — South Africa

  • Visa: Not required for UK citizens (stays up to 90 days)
  • Passport: Valid 30+ days beyond departure, 2 blank pages
  • Yellow Fever: Not needed arriving from UK
  • Malaria: Prophylaxis recommended for Sabi Sands / Leopard Hills. Sediba is malaria-free.

Health & Entry — Tanzania / Zanzibar

  • Visa: e-Visa required (US$50). Apply at eservices.immigration.go.tz
  • Passport: Valid 6+ months from entry (until at least 23 Jul 2027), 2 blank pages
  • Yellow Fever: STRONGLY RECOMMENDED — flight transits Nairobi (risk country). Must be 10+ days before travel.
  • ZIC Insurance: MANDATORY. Standard travel insurance NOT accepted.
  • Malaria: Prophylaxis required for Zanzibar.
  • Recommended vaccines: Hepatitis A & B, Typhoid, routine boosters (MMR, DTP). Book travel clinic 6–8 weeks before departure.
